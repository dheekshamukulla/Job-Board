About Us

HireWire is the ultimate job search and posting platform built just for high school students and their counselors. Whether you're looking for part-time gigs, internships, or volunteer opportunities, HireWire makes the process simple and student-friendly. With smart search tools, helpful AI chatbots, and personalized recommendations, we connect students with the right opportunities to match their interests, skills, and goals. Counselors can easily guide and support their students through the journey — all in one place.


At HireWire, we know that the job hunt can feel overwhelming—especially when you're just getting started. That’s why we designed every feature with high schoolers in mind. From resume tips to interview prep, our platform supports students at every step. Our AI chatbot is always ready to answer questions, suggest opportunities based on your goals, or walk you through the application process. Whether you’re exploring career paths or looking for your first paycheck, HireWire is here to help you shine.


For counselors, HireWire is more than a tool—it’s a partner. Our dashboard allows educators to post local jobs, track student engagement, and share resources directly. With real-time insights and curated opportunities, counselors can make a bigger impact without the extra workload. Together, we’re building a smarter, simpler path to career readiness—one student at a time.
